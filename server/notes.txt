HOW A TRANSACTION WORKS(claim)

Without a transaction:

Sarah: finds Peter (2 active rides) ← best rider
John:  finds Peter (2 active rides) ← same rider!
Sarah: assigns Peter to Ride #7
John:  assigns Peter to Ride #7
Result: Peter gets two rides at once 

With a transaction + FOR UPDATE:

Sarah: BEGIN
Sarah: finds Peter, LOCKS his row
John:  BEGIN
John:  tries to find a rider... WAITING (Peter's row is locked)
Sarah: assigns Peter to Ride #7
Sarah: COMMIT ← lock released
John:  now gets Peter... but Peter is now 'assigned'
John:  finds James instead (next best rider)
John:  assigns James to Ride #7
Result: two different riders, no double booking ✓
